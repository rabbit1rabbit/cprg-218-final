About Rayjim's Reviews

Hello, I’m Rayjim and Welcome to my game review blog! I have recently developed a interest in the game development industry and would like to eventually develop my own video game.I decided to start this blog to discover what makes a game “fun” and engaging to me. The reviews will contain a brief summary of what the game is, what I like and dislike about the game, and whether or not I recommend playing the game. My goals with these game reviews is to be able to clearly articulate my likes and dislikes in gaming.

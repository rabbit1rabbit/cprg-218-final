About Rayjim's Game Reviews

Hello, my name is Rayjim and welcome to my game review blog! I have recently developed a interest in the game development industry and would like to eventually develop my own video game.I decided to start this blog to discover what makes a game “fun” and engaging to me. The reviews will contain a brief summary of what the game is and my experiences with it, a list of the things I like in the game, and why I recommend the game. Currently, I only plan to reviewing games that I am able to recommend because I do not want to spend my time writing about what I dislike in gaming. My goals with these game reviews is to be able to clearly articulate what I like in a video game and to create a space where I am able to share my opinions on a game freely.
